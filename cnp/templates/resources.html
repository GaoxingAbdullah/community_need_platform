Food Assistance

Access to nutritious food is a basic human right. Here are some resources that can help you and your family get the food you need:

  • Food Banks

    Food banks are organizations that collect and distribute food to people in need. Many food banks also offer programs like cooking classes and nutrition education.

    Find a food bank near you

  • SNAP (Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program)

    SNAP (formerly known as food stamps) is a federal program that provides assistance to low-income individuals and families to purchase food.

    Learn more about SNAP and how to apply

  • Meals on Wheels

    Meals on Wheels is a program that delivers meals to people who are homebound due to illness, disability, or age.

    Find a Meals on Wheels provider near you

Housing Assistance

Finding and affording housing can be a challenge. Here are some resources that can help:

  • Local Organizations

    • Habitat for Humanity
    • Community Action Agency
    • Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD)

  • Government Programs

    • Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher Program
    • Low-Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP)
    • Public Housing

  • Rental Assistance

    Rental assistance programs provide financial aid to low-income individuals and families to help them afford safe and decent housing.

    Learn more about rental assistance programs

Job Training

Finding and keeping a job can be a challenge. Here are some resources that can help you acquire the skills and training you need to succeed in the workforce:

  • Job Corps

    Job Corps is a free education and training program that helps young people learn a career, earn a high school diploma or GED, and find and keep a good job.

    Learn more about Job Corps

  • Community Colleges

    Community colleges offer a wide range of job training and career development programs at a lower cost than four-year colleges and universities.

    Find a community college near you

  • Vocational Rehabilitation

    Vocational rehabilitation programs provide assistance to individuals with disabilities to help them find and keep a job.

    Learn more about vocational rehabilitation programs
